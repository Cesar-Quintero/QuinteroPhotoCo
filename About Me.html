Hey there! I’m Stephanie, but you can call me Steph!

I’m a photographer, wife to a wonderful husband, avid watcher of The Office with a healthy dose of replays of The Hobbit & Lord Of The Rings, over of the desert and mountain views of Arizona and above all else a follower of Jesus.

I have a huge heart for capturing raw, real and genuine emotions. I want my photos to reflect your life, love and the love of life with my lens. I have always had a love for photography and I love it for its capacity to freeze the sweet and fleeting moments in time, the ability to document love in all of its wild, wonderful forms and the beauty of you in all God created you to be! I’m here to tell your story.
